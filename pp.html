1. Who We Are

CHP Management is a Discord bot operated by its developer(s) (the "Operator") to support administration of a Roblox roleplay community. This Policy covers all data processed through the Bot's Discord commands, components, scheduled tasks, webhooks, direct messages, and any associated web panel.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the features described below. We do not knowingly collect payment card numbers, government identification numbers, precise geolocation, biometric data, or any other special category of data not listed in this section.

2.4 Technical / operational data

  • Command usage logs (which command was run, by whom, when, and how long it took to execute), used for debugging, abuse detection, and reliability monitoring.
  • Error reports and stack traces, which may incidentally include the Discord ID of the User who triggered the error and the command/arguments involved.
  • Consent preferences you set with the Bot (for example, whether you wish to receive end-of-shift report DMs).
  • If a companion web panel is used: authentication session data and any settings configured through it.

2.5 Information we do not intentionally collect

  • Your Discord password or Roblox password (we never have access to these — authentication is handled by Discord's and Roblox's own OAuth/verification systems).
  • Payment information (the Bot is provided free of charge; if a paid tier or panel subscription ever exists, payment is processed entirely by a third-party payment processor).
  • Precise device location or IP-based geolocation, beyond what Discord or our hosting provider may log at the infrastructure level for security purposes.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information described in Section 2 to:

  • Operate, maintain, and provide the features of the Bot as described in the Terms of Service;
  • Calculate and display shift, quota, promotion, RA/FTO, and leaderboard data accurately;
  • Send you informational direct messages you are eligible for and have not opted out of;
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, exploitation, and violations of the Terms of Service;
  • Maintain historical Roleplay Records for Server Administrators to use in legitimate staffing decisions;
  • Diagnose, debug, and improve the reliability and performance of the Bot;
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Where applicable law (such as the EU/UK GDPR) requires a legal basis for processing, we rely on: (a) performance of a contract — providing the Service you requested by using the Bot; (b) legitimate interests — operating, securing, and improving the Service, and preventing abuse; and (c) consent — for optional features such as end-of-shift report DMs, where you can opt out at any time.

5. Direct Messages

The Bot may message you directly to deliver information relevant to your own activity (such as shift reports, LOA confirmations, or RA session updates). These messages are sent only in connection with an action you or a Server Administrator initiated, and are never used for unsolicited marketing. You may be able to disable optional DM reports through the Bot's consent settings.

6. Third-Party Services & Sub-Processors

To provide the Service, we rely on the following categories of third parties, each of which processes data under its own privacy policy:

  • Discord, Inc. — the platform through which the Bot operates. See the Discord Privacy Policy.
  • Roblox Corporation — the platform hosting the connected roleplay experience. See the Roblox Privacy Policy.
  • Bloxlink and/or other Roblox-Discord account-linking providers — used to resolve a Discord-to-Roblox account mapping when you choose to link your account.
  • Database and hosting providers (e.g., MongoDB Atlas and our server/cloud hosting provider) — used to store the data described in Section 2 securely.
  • Error-monitoring / logging tooling — used to capture crash reports and diagnostic information to keep the Bot reliable.

We only share the minimum data necessary with each provider for it to perform its function, and we do not permit these providers to use your data for their own independent purposes.

7. Data Storage, Security & Retention

  • Data is stored in a cloud-hosted database and protected with reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
  • We retain Roleplay Records, configuration data, and linked-account data for as long as is reasonably necessary to provide the Service, to resolve disputes, to enforce the Terms of Service, and to comply with legal obligations.
  • If the Bot is removed from a Server, Server-specific configuration and associated records may be retained for a reasonable period in case the Server re-adds the Bot, after which it may be deleted at the Operator's discretion.
  • Command usage and error logs are retained only as long as needed for debugging and abuse investigation, and are periodically purged.

8. Data Sharing & Disclosure

Beyond the service providers described in Section 6, we may disclose your information:

  • To Server Administrators of a Server you are a member of, where the data relates to your activity within that Server;
  • Where required to comply with a valid legal request, subpoena, court order, or applicable law;
  • Where necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding suspected fraud, security issues, or violations of the Terms of Service;
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of the Operator, our Users, or the public;
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or transfer of the Bot's operation, subject to this Policy continuing to apply or the new operator providing an updated policy.

9. Server Administrator Access

By design, certain data the Bot collects about you is visible to the Server Administrators and configured staff roles of any Server you participate in, because the Bot exists to support their administration of that community. Anonymous RA feedback is not displayed with an attributed author to Server staff through normal Bot output, but is retained internally and may be reviewed by the Operator if needed to investigate abuse.

10. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not directed at children under the minimum age required by Discord's Terms of Service (currently 13). We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone below that age. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a User below the applicable minimum age, we will take reasonable steps to delete that data.

11. International Data Transfers

Our database and hosting providers may store and process data in countries other than your own. By using the Bot, you acknowledge and consent to your information being transferred to, stored in, and processed in such jurisdictions, which may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

12. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights:

  • Opt out of optional DMs (such as end-of-shift reports) using the Bot's consent command/settings, where available.
  • Request access to or deletion of your data by contacting the Operator using the details in Section 16. We will verify your identity (typically via your Discord account) before fulfilling the request.
  • Note on deletion limits: we may retain limited data where necessary to comply with a legal obligation, resolve a dispute, or enforce the Terms of Service. Deleting your account link or Roleplay Records may also mean you lose access to features that depend on that data.
  • Leaving the Service: you may stop using the Bot at any time; a Server Administrator may also remove the Bot from their Server at any time.

13. Cookies & Tracking (Panel)

If you use any companion web panel associated with the Bot, that panel may use cookies or similar local storage technology strictly necessary for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party advertising trackers on any such panel.

14. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information that creates a meaningful risk of harm, we will take reasonable steps to investigate, remediate, and notify affected Users and/or Server Administrators without undue delay, and in any event in line with applicable legal requirements.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above and, where reasonably practicable, announced through the Operator's designated community/support channels. Your continued use of the Bot after a change takes effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy.
