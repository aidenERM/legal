The Bot is provided to support administration of a Roblox roleplay community and may include, without limitation, the following functionality:

  • Duty / Shift Management — clocking on/off duty, break tracking, shift type selection, in-game nickname/callsign prefixing, quota tracking, promotion-quota tracking, weekly leaderboards, and automated shift reports.
