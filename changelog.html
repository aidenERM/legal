New Feature Update #1 · 2026-06-26 Latest

CHP Management Launch — Recruitment, Applications, /ask & a Big QOL Pass

The first full release: automatic recruitment setup, a complete in-house application system with AI-assisted review, an AI assistant that knows CHP policy, a personal /settings command, Deputy-only tools, and a long QOL pass across shifts and applications.
→ Read the full Welcome Guide for a complete tour of every feature, what's new compared to base ERM, and what each rank can do.
Automatic Setup resolves Hicom, Officer, Probationary Officer, Denied & Blacklisted roles, plus required channels — creating anything that's missing.
Passing an RA session now grants the Officer role and swaps PO→O in the trainee's nickname.
/config reorganized into focused pages (Roles, Channels & Quotas, RA Training, Recruitment) with plain-language explanations.
New application system: DM-based pre-screening (privacy policy, background check, required reading, AI warning) + 23 questions, with auto-save on timeout.
Staff can Claim, Accept, or Decline applications, run an AI Detector & Summary, check history, flag for priority, leave notes, and view a reapplication diff — all from the embed.
/ask — ask the bot anything about CHP (ranks, codes, policy, vehicles) using the official CHP knowledge base. Rate-limited to 1 req/30s bot-wide.
/profile — weekly hours vs quota, shift streak, RA pass rate, earned badges, promotion progress bar.
/dashboard — management overview combining shift, RA, and recruitment activity in one place.
Accepted applicants receive a step-by-step welcome guide instead of a plain acceptance message.
Automatic weekly report card DMs, quota reminders for anyone falling behind, and a weekly recruitment report.
/settings — personal settings: private profile, timezone, mobile-friendly embeds, opt-outs for inactivity nudges and the weekly report card.
/myactions — Deputy-only scoped view of your own recent application reviews and RA results, without full audit access.
/info — dropdown reference for 10 codes, penal/vehicle codes, loadout, vehicle regulations, and FAQ.
/practice — 5-question quiz pulled from codes and policy. Test yourself anytime.
/lookup — quickly check anyone's rank, badge number, on-duty status, and join date.
Gentler daily check-in DM for anyone inactive a few days, skips people busy training recruits.
Shift fixes: "already on duty" message, 16h+ crash auto-close, optional end-of-shift note, paginated shift history in /duty time.
Application fixes: review-and-confirm before submission, low-time warning mid-question, Apply Again if you accidentally decline the Privacy Policy step.
